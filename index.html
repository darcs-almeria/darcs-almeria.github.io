  • Climate crisis and Sustainability

    We believe that every individual and organization has a crucial role to play in tackling the pressing challenges of the climate crisis,
    and we believe in creating living spaces that contribute to a sustainable future via solar-powered homes, financial investing and onsite volunteering.

    Your home is powered by renewable energy: and it makes complete sense since it is located on one of the sunnies cities in Spain!
    Each apartment in our community is connected to state-of-the-art solar panels. Enjoy the perks of a fixed-price electricity bills
    while knowing you're reducing your environment impact on your electricity.
    We also installed energy-efficient lighting to make sure the consumption has the lowest impact as possible.
    Ready to make the move to solar-powered living? Explore our apartments and embark on a journey towards a brighter, cleaner future!

    Beach and River Cleanup: We have and will get involved in cleaning up beaches and riverbanks to prevent plastic pollution from entering the oceans,
    harming marine life, and reducing our impact on the climate crisis.
